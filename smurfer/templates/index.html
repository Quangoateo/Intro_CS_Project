OUR GROUP PROJECT

“AI Face Recognition + Website”.

When done properly, collaborative learning can be a wonderful way to motivate students, foster active learning, and develop their critical-thinking, communication, and decision-making abilities. On the other hand, for many VGU students, this is a despised topic that can be a frustrating learning experience due to a lack of applications and a website to support attendance check . To manage and display students' profiles and keep the VGU student's teammates status organized while improving the VGU student's group member search experience, we have decided to incorporate a realtime Attendance Check System into the process. In our platform, we want all of the information about students to be displayed on the website. We also want students and school administrators to have online tools that allow them to customize their directory listings to meet their own needs and references, as well as to track all of their activities through a single management system while advertising from commercial companies helps pay for the software 's maintenance and improvement. In the future, we want to invent an app for attendance check.
